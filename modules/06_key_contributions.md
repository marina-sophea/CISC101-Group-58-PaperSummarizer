Module 6 — Key Contributions Synthesizer
Short Name: Contributions Mapper Description: This module identifies the paper’s main contributions and synthesizes them into concise impact bullets. It highlights recurring themes and findings across sections, ensuring clarity and neutrality.

Inputs:

Section summaries

Raw paper text

Outputs:

Concise bullet list of key contributions and impacts

Key Constraints/Checks:

Contributions must be traceable to paper content

Neutral academic tone

No external information introduced
